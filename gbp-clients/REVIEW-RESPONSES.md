# GBP Review Response Templates

## 5-Star Reviews

> Hi [Name], thank you so much for the kind words! It was a pleasure working on [mention specific detail from review]. We take pride in [relevant value] and it means a lot to hear it made a difference. Give us a shout anytime you need us again!

## 4-Star Reviews

> Thanks for the review, [Name]! Glad we could help with [mention detail]. We're always looking to improve — is there anything we could have done to make it a 5-star experience? We'd love to earn that extra star next time. 📞 081 391 7733

## 3-Star Reviews

> Hi [Name], thank you for your honest feedback. We're sorry [mention specific issue from review if any]. We take this seriously and would like to make it right. Please give us a call at [phone] — we'd really appreciate the chance to fix this.

## 1-2 Star Reviews

> Hi [Name], we're genuinely sorry to hear about your experience. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. We'd really like to understand what went wrong and make it right. Please contact us directly at [phone] or [email] at your earliest convenience. We take every review seriously and want to earn back your trust.

## Tips
- Always respond within 24 hours
- Never get defensive — even if the review is unfair
- Use the customer's name if provided
- Mention something specific from their review so it doesn't sound copy-pasted
- For negative reviews: take it offline ("call us at...")
- For positive reviews: mention a specific detail and reinforce your value
