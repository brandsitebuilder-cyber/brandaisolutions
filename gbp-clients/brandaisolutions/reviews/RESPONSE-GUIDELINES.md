# Review Response Guidelines — Brand AI Solutions

## Brand Voice
- **Style:** Professional, modern, and confident. Not salesy — helpful.
- **Tone:** Warm but direct. "We build websites that work" energy.
- **Key phrases:** "digital presence", "visual-first", "lead-generating"
- **Avoid:** Overly formal, corporate jargon, overly casual slang
- **Language:** English only

## Response Rules (ALL reviews)
1. Always use the reviewer's name if visible
2. Mention something specific from their review
3. Keep it 2-4 sentences — concise and genuine
4. Never sound copy-pasted or templated
5. Never get defensive (even for bad reviews)
6. Include contact info for negative reviews: marcus@brandaisolutions.co.za

## 5-Star Responses
- Thank them warmly
- Mention something specific they praised
- Reinforce that we're here if they need anything
- Example vibe: "Thanks so much [Name]! Really glad you loved [specific thing]. We're always here if you need anything else."

## 4-Star Responses
- Thank them
- Acknowledge the positive feedback
- Ask what would make it 5 stars (gently)
- Example vibe: "Thanks [Name]! Appreciate the kind words about [thing]. Always looking to improve — let us know if there's anything we could do better."

## 3-Star Responses
- Thank them for the feedback
- Acknowledge their concern specifically
- Offer to make it right
- Provide direct contact: marcus@brandaisolutions.co.za
- Example vibe: "Thanks for the honest feedback, [Name]. I hear you on [specific issue] and I'd love to make this right. Please reach out at marcus@brandaisolutions.co.za — let's sort it out."

## 1-2 Star Responses
- Sincere apology (even if you disagree)
- Acknowledge the specific issue
- Take it offline — provide email
- Brief, professional, not defensive
- Example vibe: "I'm really sorry to hear about your experience, [Name]. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. I'd really appreciate the chance to make things right — please email me at marcus@brandaisolutions.co.za and I'll handle it personally."

## Email Notification (for 1-3 star reviews)
When a negative review is detected, send an email to marcus@brandaisolutions.co.za with:
- Subject: "⚠️ New {star}-Star Review — Brand AI Solutions"
- Body includes: reviewer name, star rating, full review text, AI-generated draft response
- Action required: Marcus reviews the draft and posts the response
