# Review Response Guidelines — Eltec Electrical

## Brand Voice
- **Style:** Professional but approachable. Confident. "Your Partner in Power."
- **Tone:** Straight-talking professional who takes pride in neat work. Not flashy — just really good at what they do.
- **Key phrases:** "safe, code-compliant", "on time, on budget", "qualified electricians", "Certificate of Compliance"
- **Avoid:** Overly casual, slang, defensive language
- **Language:** English only

## Response Rules (ALL reviews)
1. Always use the reviewer's name if visible
2. Mention something specific from their review
3. Keep it 2-4 sentences — concise and genuine
4. Never sound copy-pasted or templated
5. Never get defensive (even for bad reviews)
6. Include contact info for negative reviews: info@eltec.co.za / 081 391 7733

## 5-Star Responses
- Thank them warmly
- Mention something specific they praised (reliability, speed, neatness, professionalism)
- Reinforce that they can call anytime
- Example vibe: "Thanks so much [Name]! Really appreciate the kind words about [specific thing]. We take pride in delivering safe, code-compliant work. Give us a shout anytime you need us — 081 391 7733."

## 4-Star Responses
- Thank them
- Acknowledge the positive feedback
- Ask what would make it 5 stars (gently)
- Example vibe: "Thanks [Name]! Glad we could help with [thing]. We're always looking to improve — is there anything we could have done better? We'd love to earn that extra star next time."

## 3-Star Responses
- Thank them for the feedback
- Acknowledge their concern specifically
- Offer to make it right
- Provide direct contact: info@eltec.co.za / 081 391 7733
- Example vibe: "Thanks for the honest feedback, [Name]. We hear you on [specific issue] and take it seriously. Please give us a call at 081 391 7733 — we'd really appreciate the chance to make this right."

## 1-2 Star Responses
- Sincere apology (even if you disagree)
- Acknowledge the specific issue
- Take it offline — provide phone and email
- Brief, professional, not defensive
- Example vibe: "We're genuinely sorry to hear about your experience, [Name]. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. We'd really like to understand what went wrong and make it right. Please contact us directly at 081 391 7733 or info@eltec.co.za — we take every review seriously."

## Email Notification (for 1-3 star reviews)
When a negative review is detected, send an email to marcus@brandaisolutions.co.za with:
- Subject: "⚠️ New {star}-Star Review — Eltec Electrical"
- Body includes: reviewer name, star rating, full review text, AI-generated draft response
- Action required: Marcus reviews the draft and posts the response
