## Post 1: Is Your Geyser Eating Your Electricity Budget? 💸

Here's something most homeowners don't realise: your geyser accounts for roughly 40% of your monthly electricity bill. In winter, with colder inlet water, that number climbs even higher.

A simple geyser timer can cut that cost significantly. Instead of keeping water at 65°C all day and night, the timer heats it for a few hours in the morning and evening — exactly when you need it. You won't notice the difference in your shower, but you will notice it on your municipal account.

We install geyser timers, blankets, and energy-efficient thermostats. It's a small job that pays for itself within months.
