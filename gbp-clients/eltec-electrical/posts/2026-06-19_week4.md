## Post 1: 3 Signs Your DB Board Needs a Check-Up
Winter in the Northern Suburbs means everyone's heater is running at full tilt — and that puts real strain on your distribution board. Here's what to look out for: (1) your lights flicker when you switch on a high-draw appliance like a heater or kettle, (2) circuits trip more than once a month, or (3) you catch a faint burning smell near the board. 🔥 Any one of these is a sign your DB board isn't coping with the load, and ignoring it could leave you in the dark on a cold evening. A quick inspection now costs far less than an emergency call-out later. Stay warm, stay safe. ⚡🔧

## Post 3: Keeping Bellville Warm and Safe This Winter
Nothing beats a cosy lounge on a cold Cape winter's evening 🏠 — but those extra heaters and electric blankets also mean your home's electrical system is working overtime. Here in Bellville, Durbanville, and Brackenfell, we see the same thing every winter: overloaded circuits, old plug points struggling, and extension leads snaking everywhere. A quick tip: if your plug point feels warm to the touch, unplug something immediately. Better yet, let one of our qualified electricians do a winter safety check on your home.
