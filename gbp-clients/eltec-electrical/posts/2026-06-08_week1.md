## Post 3: Winter Electrical Safety Check — Durbanville & Bellville 🏠

Winter in the Cape means one thing: everything electric gets pushed harder. Heaters come out of storage. Geysers run longer. Electric blankets get dusted off.

Before you fire everything up, do a quick safety sweep:

- Check heater cords for fraying or damage (rodents love stored cables)
- Test your earth leakage — press that little test button on your DB board
- Smell burning? Switch off immediately and call us
- Geyser thermostat — if your water is suddenly SCALDING hot, it's failing

## Post 4: Solar-Ready DB Boards — Future-Proof Your Home ☀️

Solar is the conversation every homeowner is having right now. But here's the thing most people miss: your DB board needs to be ready for it.

A standard domestic DB board isn't designed for solar integration. You need proper split neutrals, surge protection, and space for the changeover switch. Install solar panels on an unprepared board and you're looking at nuisance tripping, overloads, and potentially dangerous faults.
